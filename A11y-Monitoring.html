Our Accessibility Monitoring Platform, developed by PopeTech and powered by the trusted WAVE engine, is designed for in-house development teams and digital agencies that manage multiple websites. This platform ensures ongoing compliance with WCAG, Section 508, and local accessibility standards, allowing your team to focus on building accessible content from the start. Supported by DHS Certified Trusted Testers, this monitoring service provides comprehensive coverage and actionable insights to help you maintain an accessible web presence.

The platform comes with support by A11y Developers that are DHS Certified Trusted Testers to assist as needed and a Proof of Compliance (POC) Certificate providing transparent documentation that verifies your website’s commitment to accessibility. This certificate serves as a powerful asset, communicating to visitors, stakeholders, and clients that your site meets legal accessibility requirements and adheres to ADA standards.

Key Features of the Accessibility Monitoring Platform

  • Unlimited Access: Supports unlimited users, websites, groups, and rescanning capabilities, allowing you to cover every aspect of your digital portfolio.
  • Custom Page Limits: Plans are tailored to accommodate your specific needs, ensuring flexibility across large or small websites.

  • Comprehensive Scanning: Includes the ability to scan behind logins, templates, co-branded pages, Single Sign-On (SSO), and more. This flexibility is crucial for evaluating complex, secure web environments.
  • On-Demand and Scheduled Scans: Set up automated, scheduled scans to monitor accessibility continuously, or trigger on-demand scans when updates are made.

  • Hands-On Support: We provide personalized onboarding and detailed training sessions to help your team fully leverage the platform’s features, ensuring a smooth transition and effective use of monitoring tools.

  • Professional Support: Our experts are available via email and Zoom to assist your team with any accessibility queries, providing reliable guidance and solutions.

  • Regular Updates: Receive monthly reports detailing accessibility performance and focus on specific topics, such as alternative text, keyboard navigation, and color contrast.
  • Built-In Training Resources: Access unlimited user resources, including training materials related to each month’s focus, helping your team stay informed and up-to-date.

  • Inclusive Training: All plans include five training seats that grant access to live, online accessibility training sessions, quarterly webinars, and our annual accessibility conference.
  • Accessibility Help Desk: Reach out to our help desk specialists for guidance on specific accessibility issues and test results.

  • Scheduled Manual Testing: In addition to automated scans, schedule and track manual testing sessions to ensure thorough accessibility coverage, with notes and results integrated into your regular reports.
