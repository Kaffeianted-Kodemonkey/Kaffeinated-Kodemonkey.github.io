Why POUR Principles Matter in Web Design

With POUR Web Design, we don’t just focus on compliance—we prioritize a user-centered approach that reaches a wider audience. Building with Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, and Robust principles ensures your site is inclusive, accessible, and aligned with today’s best practices. Not only is POUR Web Design the right thing to do, but it also supports your business goals by expanding your reach and increasing user satisfaction.

How POUR Web Design Works

We make sure that every element on your site can be easily perceived by all users:

  • Intuitive Layouts: Accessible color contrasts, legible fonts, and an easy-to-navigate structure make your site welcoming.
  • Alternative Text: Images, videos, and interactive elements are supported with descriptive text to ensure accessibility.
  • Content Accessibility: We prioritize readability and clarity to make content accessible across all devices.

We simplify your message and design, making it easy for all users to understand and engage:

  • Keyboard Navigation: Clear and consistent paths enable easy access to all areas of your site.
  • Interactive Optimization: Forms, buttons, and links are optimized for effortless interaction.

We simplify your message and design, making it easy for all users to understand and engage:

  • Clear Language: Simple, user-friendly language enhances comprehension.
  • Guided Navigation: Helpful cues and instructions make interaction straightforward.
  • Consistent Design: A cohesive layout ensures an intuitive experience from page to page.

We future-proof your website to support a broad range of users and technologies:

  • Standards-Compliant Code: Clean, valid code ensures compatibility with assistive technologies.
  • Cross-Platform Compatibility: Rigorous testing across devices and browsers guarantees accessibility for all users.
  • Assistive Technology Support: Functionality is optimized to work with various assistive devices.

POUR Web Design Options

We offer two flexible approaches to creating a fully accessible website that enhances user experience and meets ADA compliance:

  • Custom-Built Websites: These websites are crafted from the ground up to fully integrate POUR (Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, and Robust) principles, ensuring an end-to-end ADA-compliant product.
  • CMS Platforms: We work with your preferred content management system (CMS) or recommend the best option to meet your needs, ensuring accessibility features are seamlessly integrated. Please note that not all CMS platforms are ADA-compliant. While our A11y Developers will ensure accessibility is prioritized, we do not redesign or rebuild modules, plugins, or themes.

Both options come with a one-year Proof of Compliance (POC) Certificate to show your commitment to accessibility.
