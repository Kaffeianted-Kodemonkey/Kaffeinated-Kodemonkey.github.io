Proof Of Compliance (POC) Certification

The POC Certification outlines the endeavors to become ADA Compliant. Our team has engaged a DHS Certified A11y Developer that leverages the DHS Trusted Tested process while following the WCAG/508 2.1 guidelines for Level AA.

Due to code revisions, documents, and standard updates, it is nearly impossible to reach 100% compliance. Therefore our team is committed to working with an Kaffeinated Kodemonkey's A11y Developer to monitor accessibility and stay compliant.

Certification Process

The certification process consists of an A11y evaluation and remediation of issues.

  1. A11y Evaluation Audit: This is to obtain the current A11y Status and set the base understanding of accessibility.
  2. POC Certification: Consists of the following: A certification - displaying current compliance level, evaluation version, risk score, expiration date, and TTID #; A11y Statement - depiction of unresolved accessibility issues along any third-party elements that are not compliant with a path to resolution.
  3. A11y Monitoring: Kaffeinated Kodemonkey A11y Developer monitors the accessibility of the site, resolves issues, and updates all documents as required.
