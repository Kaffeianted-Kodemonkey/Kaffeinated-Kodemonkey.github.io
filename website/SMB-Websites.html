Wheather you use a CMS or the SIPA Portal, we follow WCAG principals to make your website accessible to everyone:

  • Perceivable - Users must be able to perceive the information being presented
  • Operable - Users must be able to operate the interface easily
  • Understandable - Users must be able to understand and operation of the user interface
  • Robust - Users must be able to access the content as technologies advance
* Websites are not ADA compliant out of the box, you will need to design for accessiblity. Our experts can help you with this.
