Invest in a Web Accessibility Audit

A web accessibility audit ensures compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), and Section 508, reducing legal risks and improving user experience. Over 8,800 ADA Title III lawsuits targeted websites in 2024, with fines up to $75,000 for first violations (Seyfarth Shaw, 2024; DOJ, 2014). Accessibility also unlocks a $2.6 trillion disposable income market held by people with disabilities (Return on Disability Group, 2024).

Our DHS Certified Trusted Testers use manual testing (e.g., NVDA, VoiceOver) and tools like WAVE and axe to identify barriers for users with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive impairments, ensuring WCAG 2.2 Level AA compliance, including new criteria like focus visibility (2.4.11) and accessible authentication (3.3.8). Audits boost SEO rankings through keyword-rich alt text, semantic HTML, and mobile-friendly design, aligning with Google’s Core Web Vitals. Clients see 15-25% organic traffic growth in 3 months (HubSpot, 2023).
