What the deliverables actually mean

Proof of Compliance Certificate (POCC)

A branded, public-facing certificate and seal you can display on your site stating it has been manually tested by a DHS Certified Trusted Tester and conforms to WCAG 2.X AA (or the level achieved). Perfect for marketing, building user trust, and satisfying most private-sector due-diligence Accessibility requirements.

Section 508 Accessibility Report (SAR)

The complete, procurement-ready report required by federal agencies, most state governments, higher-education institutions, and large enterprises. Includes a formal cover letter, executive summary, testing methodology, annotated findings with screenshots and code examples, and a signed certification page from a DHS Certified Trusted Tester.
