What is a Proof of Compliance Certificate (POCC)?

A Proof of Compliance Certificate (POCC) verifies that your digital assets — websites, applications, or documents — meet standards like Section 508 Compliance and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). Our process includes comprehensive website accessibility audits, detailed reporting, and a certificate validating compliance. This credential supports regulatory requirements, procurement processes, or public accountability, including alignment with Colorado’s HB21-1110 for government entities. For RFP needs, start with a VPAT Certificate and progress to a POCC for full certification, ensuring compliance with confidence.
