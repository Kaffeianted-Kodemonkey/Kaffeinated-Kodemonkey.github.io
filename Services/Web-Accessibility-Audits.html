Web A11y Audit

Our web accessibility audit is a streamlined to be tailored for any digital project across industries. Conducted by a DHS Certified Trusted Tester, it ensures compliance with WCAG 2.1/2.2 Level A and AA, and Section 508, while enhancing user inclusivity and SEO.

Our A11y team test against POUR principles (Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust), covering alt text, keyboard navigation, color contrast, video captions, and WCAG 2.2 criteria like target size (2.5.8), dragging movements (2.5.7), and accessible authentication (3.3.8). Manual testing with accessible tools and assitive technology ensure accuracy across devices, including mobile and assistive technologies.

  • Proof of Compliance Certificate (POCC): A formal document with a detailed report of issues, fixes, and compliance status, demonstrating good-faith efforts under ADA Titles II/III and Section 508.
  • Disability Impact Score: A proprietary metric prioritizing fixes that maximize inclusivity for diverse users.
  • Actionable Remediation Plan: Clear steps to resolve issues, with optional remediation services to achieve full compliance.
  • SEO Recommendations: Keyword-rich alt text and structured HTML to improve rankings for terms like “web accessibility audit.”

VPAT Only

Our Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) is a standalone offering for organizations needing precise, compliance-ready documentation without a full audit. A VPAT details your website, app, or digital product’s conformance with WCAG 2.1/2.2 and Section 508, making it essential for federal contractors, government RFPs, or procurement processes. Our DHS Certified Trusted Testers conduct a focused evaluation to complete the VPAT, assessing key accessibility features like keyboard navigation, alt text, and new WCAG 2.2 criteria (e.g., 2.5.7 Dragging Movements, 3.3.8 Accessible Authentication).

  • Accuracy and Speed: Delivered in 3-5 days, our VPATs are thorough, reflecting your product’s accessibility status with detailed conformance notes.
  • xProcurement Advantage: Meets federal and state requirements (e.g., Section 508, EAA), increasing your chances of winning contracts.
  • Customized Scope: We tailor VPATs for specific products (e.g., websites, apps, PDFs) or entire systems, ensuring relevance for your RFP.
  • Path to Full Compliance: A VPAT can be your first step toward a comprehensive POCC, with optional audit upgrades to address identified issues.
  • Expert Support: Includes a consultation to explain findings and next steps, ensuring you understand compliance gaps.

Invest in a Web Accessibility Audit

A web accessibility audit ensures compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), and Section 508, reducing legal risks and improving user experience. Over 8,800 ADA Title III lawsuits targeted websites in 2024, with fines up to $75,000 for first violations (Seyfarth Shaw, 2024; DOJ, 2014). Accessibility also unlocks a $2.6 trillion disposable income market held by people with disabilities (Return on Disability Group, 2024).

Our DHS Certified Trusted Testers use manual testing (e.g., NVDA, VoiceOver) and tools like WAVE and axe to identify barriers for users with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive impairments, ensuring WCAG 2.2 Level AA compliance, including new criteria like focus visibility (2.4.11) and accessible authentication (3.3.8). Audits boost SEO rankings through keyword-rich alt text, semantic HTML, and mobile-friendly design, aligning with Google’s Core Web Vitals. Clients see 15-25% organic traffic growth in 3 months (HubSpot, 2023).
