What is a Proof of Compliance Certificate (POCC)?

A Proof of Compliance Certificate (POCC) from Kaffeinated Kodemonkey, backed by DHS Trusted Tester audits, verifies ADA, WCAG 2.2, and Section 508 compliance, ensuring your digital platforms are inclusive and legally compliant for all users.

Benefits of the POCC

  • Builds Trust: Demonstrates your commitment to accessibility, boosting credibility with stakeholders.
  • Reduces Legal Risks: Meets ADA, WCAG 2.2, and Section 508 standards, minimizing litigation exposure.
  • Boosts Visibility: Enhances SEO through accessible design, attracting more users to your platform.
  • Promotes Inclusivity: Ensures equitable digital access for all, supporting diverse audiences.
