What is a POC Certificate?

The Proof of Compliance (POC) Certificate from Kaffeinated Kodemonkey is a trusted verification that your website meets essential WCAG and Section 508 standards, proving that your digital content is accessible to people with disabilities. This certification reflects your commitment to accessibility and inclusive design, helping businesses, educational institutions, and government agencies demonstrate their dedication to equal access.

Displaying a POC Certificate on your website offers more than just legal compliance. It communicates a proactive stance on inclusivity and best practices in accessibility, reassuring visitors, clients, and stakeholders of your organization’s commitment to digital accessibility.

Benefits of the POC Certificate

  • Builds Trust and Credibility: The POC Certificate signals your dedication to accessibility, enhancing brand trust and credibility among users and clients.
  • Reduces Legal Risks: Meeting ADA and WCAG compliance helps safeguard your organization from potential legal issues.
