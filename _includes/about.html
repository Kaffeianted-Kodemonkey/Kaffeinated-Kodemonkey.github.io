About Us

Helping Agencies & Companies become ADA compliant and maintain WCAG/508 Status.

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey is an Accessibility Agency

Our A11y Developers are DHS Certified Trusted Testers and use the trusted tester process. Paired with an A11y Platform, empower your IT teams to seamlessly add accessibility to their workflow.

Our A11y Developer also follow POUR web design, for an A11y-First approach in creating products that meet WCAG/508 requirements.

  • Perceivable - Users must be able to perceive the information being presented
  • Operable - Users must be able to operate the interface
  • Understandable - Users must be able to understand information & operations
  • Robust - Users must be able to access the content as technologies advances

This approach can improve productivity, ROI, saves time and money, and help ward off potential lawsuits when accessibility is include from the start.

As the owner of Kaffeinated Kodemonkey, I am devoted to helping businesses meet WCAG and Section 508 criteia. Out Accessibility Developers are DHS Certified Trusted Testers and use the Trusted Tester process.

With a background in the corporate and startup sectors, as a front-end developer, the owner picked up Web Accessibility along the way, assisted in the development of technical documents, and education of the staff on the importance of accessibility on the website.

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey grew into an accessibility agency offering VPAT completion, Accessibility Audits, and WCAG/508 Evaluations.
