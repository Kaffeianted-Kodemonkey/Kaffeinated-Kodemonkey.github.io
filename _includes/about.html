Our A11y Developers are DHS Certified Trusted Testers and use the trusted tester process. Paired with an A11y platform, your teams can add web accessibility to their workflow with ease.

Our A11y team follows POUR web design, for an A11y-First approach in design and evaluation audits.

  • Perceivable - Users must be able to perceive the information being presented
  • Operable - Users must be able to operate the interface
  • Understandable - Users must be able to understand information & operations
  • Robust - Users must be able to access the content as technology advances

This approach can improve ROI through on-site SEO and help ward off potential lawsuits involving accessibility compliance.
