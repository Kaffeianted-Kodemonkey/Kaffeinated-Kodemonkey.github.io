Section 508 compliance is a U.S. federal law requiring federal agencies to ensure their electronic and information technology (ICT) is accessible to people with disabilities. This applies to technology that is developed, maintained, procured, or used by the federal government and includes everything from websites and software to electronic documents and hardware. To achieve compliance, organizations must make sure their systems can be operated in a variety of ways, not relying on a single sense or ability, and that they work with accessibility tools like screen readers.
