  • 02 What is WCAG & Section 508?

    Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG): Acessibility guidelines by the Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI) of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), the international standards organization for the Internet.

    Section 508: Federal agencies are required to make Information and Communications Technology (ICT) (online training and websites) accessible to everyone with or without a disability. Everyone should be able to do their work/training/ or access the agency’s internal website on accessible devices. This also applies to people with or without a disability applying for a job with the federal government or a person who is using an agency’s website to get information about a program or completing an online form and has access to the same access information and resources available to anyone.

  • 03 What is a VPAT/ARC?

    Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT®): Explains how ICT products such as software, hardware, electronic content, and support documentation meet Secton 508 standards for IT accessibility. The VPAT® is a templated document used by TTADs to create an ACR for all businesses that create ICT products.

    Accessibility Conformance Report (ACR): Is based on the ITI VPAT® the ACR demonstratinos the compliance leve of the product.
