What is a WiFi hotspot?

A hotspot is a physical location (powered by a Wi-Fi device) that enables free guest WiFi where people may obtain internet access. This is done through a wireless local area network (WLAN) using a router (access point) connected to an internet service provider. Zenreach is a hardware agnostic platform available on most routers.

How does Zenreach actually work for my customers and guests?
  1. Zenreach enables you to provide your customers with free guest WiFi.
  2. Customers can then access this secure WiFi network through your branded portal.
  3. Zenrach collects customer contact information and keeps track of how often your customers come back through your door.
  4. You can then automate smart marketing to your customers who log onto WiFi.
  5. Zenreach shows merchants how many people walk back through the door after receiving a message (our Walk-Through Rate™) and calculates incremental revenue and marketing attribution.
