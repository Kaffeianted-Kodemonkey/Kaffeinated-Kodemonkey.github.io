Kaffeinated Kodemonkey strives for accessibility on all sites. Our focus is on helping rural communities and local government excel online with websites that meet the WCAG and Section 508 standards.

By leveraging a platform developed with accessibility baked in from the ground up you can rest asure that your site will preform on any assitive technology and be resposive right out of the box. This allows us to focus on making the layout and content accessible. Saving you time and money.

Why do we do this?

We don't want you to loos business because someone cannot access your site due to a disability.

Accessible site organitcly gain the following benifets:

  • Helps with SEO ranking
  • Drive traffice to your site
  • Retains site visitors

Just as a brick an mortar store has to follow ADA guidelines so do online sites and docuemnts. By having an accessible site you increase your SEO ratings and avoid singling out potential customers.
