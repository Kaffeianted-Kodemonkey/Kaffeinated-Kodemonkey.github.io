Unlock the Power of the POC Certificate

The POC Certificate serves as a compilation of documents that validate your website's adherence to ADA regulations. It encompasses websites, kiosks, applications, PDFs, and all online content classified as ICT (Information and Communications Technology) falling under Title III. This title strictly prohibits discrimination based on disability in "places of public accommodation." As businesses increasingly shift towards digital platforms, ADA regulations are continually evolving to ensure parity in experiences both online and offline.

Acquiring a POC Certificate serves as an explicit demonstration to your site visitors that you prioritize accessibility and strive to offer an exceptional experience to all users. Moreover, it provides documented evidence of your product's compliance level, outlines how your company handles non-compliance issues, and offers guidance on whom to contact in case users encounter challenges while accessing or navigating your product.
