What is a Proof of Compliance (POC) Certificate?

A Proof of Compliance (POC) Certificate is an official validation that your website meets essential ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) and WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) standards. This certification ensures compliance, while boosting usability and accessibility across all devices, making your digital space inclusive to all users.

Key Takeaway

  • WCAG Criteria: Your website will meet WCAG 2.2 standards, ensuring accessibility for a global audience.
  • Section 508 Compliance: We ensure full compliance with Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act, crucial for federal agencies and public-facing organizations.
  • State and Local ADA Compliance: Our tailored approach addresses specific state and local ADA regulations, ensuring comprehensive regional compliance.
