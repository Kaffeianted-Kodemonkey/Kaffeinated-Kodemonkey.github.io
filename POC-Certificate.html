Certification Overview

The POC Certification outlines Kaffeinated Kodemonkey's endeavors to become ADA Compliant and meet WCAG/508 2.1 Level AA requirements and offer supporting documents.

Kaffienated Kodemonkey is a DHS Certified A11y Developer that leverages the DHS Trusted Tested process while following the WCAG/508 2.1 guidelines for Level AA.

Due to code revisions, documents, and standard updates, it is nearly impossible to reach 100% compliance. Therefore our team is committed to working with a Certified A11y Developer to monitor accessibility and remain compliant.

POC Certification is comprised of following:

  1. A11y Statement - Depiction of unresolved accessibility issues including third-party elements that are not compliant with a path to resolution.
  2. A11y Audit Report: Results form the a11y audit; returns a lists of accessibility issues and sets current compliant status.
  3. A11y Compliance Report: Details results from a Manual Evaluation verifying each WCAG criteria status.

A11y Monitoring

To remain in compliance our Certified A11y Developer will monitor the site, resolves issues, and updates all documents as to reflect any changes in compliance.
