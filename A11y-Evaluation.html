A Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) is a document that evaluates how accessible a particular product is according to the WCAG & Section 508 Standards. It is a self-disclosing document that details each aspect of the WCAG & Section 508 requirements and how the product supports the criteria. The VPAT documents use the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) for criteria requirements. All sections of a website must meet the conformances level of AA. In the UK, you will need to follow the European Accessibility standards EN 301.
