A Voluntary Product Accessibility Template is a document that evaluates how accessible a particular product is according ada compliance standards. It is a self-disclosing document that details each aspect of the WCAG requirements and how the product is supported.

This documents use the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) for criteria requirements. All federal and local government websites must meet WCAG conformances level of AA. In the UK, you will need to follow the European Accessibility standards EN 301.

When a company attempts to complete the VPAT in-house, they tend to miss issues or misinterpret the WCAG and Section 508 requirements. VPATs are complex and confusing, most times those designated to handle them have not even heard of web accessibility let alone the WCAG requirements and ADA Compliace.

Completing the VPAT can be frustrating unless you have an accessibility developer on hand. When looking for a third party to assist your team with accessibility requirements, consider these things before hiring:

  • Make sure they do manual testing and not just automated testing; as there is a lot that can be missing through automation alone.
  • Check credentials - What memberships do they hold, and what certifications do they have?
