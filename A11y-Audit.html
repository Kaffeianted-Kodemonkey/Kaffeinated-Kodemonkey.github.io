A Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) is a self-disclosing document that details how a product aligns with the WCAG criteria. It provides insights into the product’s level of accessibility through a comprehensive Web Accessibility Audit and Evaluation. Completing a VPAT allows you to assess and document your website's current accessibility status.

A VPAT is a great way to demonstrate your site's current compliance level and can be instrumental in gaining buy-in for a full compliance audit. It effectively highlights any existing issues and facilitates conversations with management or shareholders about the importance of becoming ADA Compliant. For government entities, compliance with Section 508 is mandatory, making Accessibility Evaluation and VPAT documentation essential.

Notably: VPATs are often required in Requests for Proposals (RFPs) to ensure that products meet specific accessibility standards.

Importance of Compliance

WCAG serves as the benchmark for web accessibility standards. Federal and local government websites must conform to WCAG Level AA. Similarly, the European Accessibility standards EN 301 apply in the UK. A VPAT Certificate is often requested as part of RFPs or contracts to ensure compliance.
