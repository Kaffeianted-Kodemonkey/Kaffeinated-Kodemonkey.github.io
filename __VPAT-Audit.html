What is a VPAT Audit

A Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) Audit is your key to proving ADA compliance for contracts and procurement. Our process assesses your website against WCAG 2.2 and Section 508 guidelines, delivering a detailed report that highlights accessibility strengths and gaps. Whether you’re bidding on a government project or ensuring regulatory alignment, our VPAT Audit sets the stage for actionable improvements.

How It Works:

  • Auto Scan: Cutting-edge tools quickly scan your site to identify accessibility issues.
  • Manual Evaluation: Our DHS Certified Trusted Testers dive deeper, manually reviewing elements that automated tools might miss.
  • Current VPAT Completion: We deliver an up-to-date VPAT document tailored to your site, ready for RFPs, contracts, or internal use.
