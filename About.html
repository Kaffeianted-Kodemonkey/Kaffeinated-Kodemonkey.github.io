Who We Are

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey is a small but mighty web accessibility agency with a big mission: to make the digital world more inclusive and accessible for everyone. Founded by a dedicated advocate for accessibility, we help businesses and organizations create websites and applications that meet the highest standards of accessibility. Our goal is simple yet powerful—ensuring that the web is a space where everyone, regardless of their abilities, can engage fully and without barriers.

At the heart of our mission is a commitment to nurturing the next generation of A11y Developers and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs). Through our hands-on mentorship program, we empower emerging talents with the skills, knowledge, and real-world experience they need to become leaders in the field of digital accessibility.

Our Services

As a small business, we take pride in offering personalized, hands-on service across a range of areas, including:

  • Accessibility Audits: Comprehensive reviews of your digital properties to identify and fix accessibility issues.
  • Proof of Compliance Certification: Helping you meet WCAG standards and providing certification to showcase your commitment to accessibility.
  • Consultation and Training: Tailored advice and training sessions to empower your team with the knowledge and skills needed to prioritize accessibility.
  • Web Development and Design: Building and refining websites and apps with accessibility as a core principle, ensuring usability for all.

Mentorship Program

At Kaffeinated Kodemonkey, we’re not just passionate about web accessibility; we’re also committed to giving back. Our mentorship program is designed to provide students and emerging talents with real-world experience in the field of web accessibility. We offer our mentees the chance to work on live projects, gaining practical skills and building portfolios that will help them succeed in their careers.

Our small size allows us to provide personalized guidance, ensuring that each mentee receives the support and mentorship they need to grow. We believe in fostering a mindset of inclusivity and empathy, helping our mentees develop not just technical skills, but also a deep understanding of the importance of accessibility in the digital world.

Our Community

As a small business, community is at the heart of what we do. We’ve created The ChatterRoom, an online forum where our team, clients, and community members can connect, share knowledge, and collaborate. Whether you’re looking for advice, want to discuss the latest trends in accessibility, or simply want to connect with others who share your passion, The ChatterRoom is the place for you.

Why Choose Us?

When you choose Kaffeinated Kodemonkey, you’re choosing a small business that’s big on commitment, expertise, and heart. We combine our deep knowledge of web accessibility with a genuine passion for helping our clients succeed. Our personalized approach means that we work closely with you to ensure your digital spaces are not just compliant, but truly accessible to everyone.

Join us in our mission to create a more inclusive web—one site, one app, and one line of code at a time.
