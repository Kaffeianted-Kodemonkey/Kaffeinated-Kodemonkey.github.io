Who We Are

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey is a specialized web accessibility agency dedicated to making the digital world accessible for everyone. Founded by a seasoned web developer with a strong background in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Ruby on Rails, Kaffeinated Kodemonkey brings technical expertise and a commitment to creating ADA-compliant websites that prioritize accessibility.

Our History

The journey began in 2015 when our founder encountered a web accessibility document while working on a project with a college. This document, intended to outline the college’s commitment to accessibility, was over three years old and had never been implemented. Although the college considered the document sufficient despite its outdated state, this response sparked our founder’s interest and concern about web accessibility. Driven to go beyond “good enough,” our founder attempted to implement accessibility changes but received significant pushback. This experience ultimately motivated them to expand from freelance development into establishing an agency focused on accessibility and ADA compliance.

Today, Kaffeinated Kodemonkey is a trusted partner in web accessibility, providing in-depth site evaluations for ADA compliance and developing accessible web solutions across diverse industries.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful—to ensure the web is a barrier-free space where all users, regardless of ability, can fully engage. Our hands-on mentorship program also supports this mission by nurturing the next generation of A11y Developers and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs), equipping emerging talents with skills, experience, and the dedication to become future leaders in digital accessibility.

Our Services

As a small but focused agency, we offer highly personalized service across key areas:

  • Accessibility Audits: Comprehensive evaluations to identify and resolve accessibility barriers.
  • Proof of Compliance Certification: Certifying websites to meet WCAG standards, with documentation to showcase commitment.
  • Consultation and Training: Customized guidance to empower teams with accessibility knowledge.
  • Web Development and Design: Designing and building ADA-focused websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Ruby on Rails for user-friendly, compliant digital experiences.

Mentorship Program

We’re passionate about web accessibility and dedicated to giving back. Through our mentorship program, we provide students and rising professionals with real-world experience in accessibility. By working on live projects, mentees gain practical skills, build portfolios, and learn to champion accessibility in digital spaces.

Our Community

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey is more than an agency; it’s a community. Through The ChatterRoom, our online forum, our team, clients, and community members connect, share knowledge, and collaborate. It’s a space to seek advice, explore accessibility trends, and connect with others who share a commitment to a more accessible web.

Why Choose Kaffeinated Kodemonkey?

When you partner with us, you’re choosing a small agency with a big heart, technical expertise, and deep knowledge of accessibility. Our background in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Ruby on Rails, combined with our specialized focus on ADA compliance, enables us to provide an experience that’s both personal and professional. We work closely with our clients to ensure their digital spaces are accessible, compliant, and built for all users.
