Our Mission

At Kaffeinated Kodemonkey, we are on a mission to make the digital world more accessible to everyone. We believe that inclusivity should be at the heart of web development, and we strive to empower businesses and organizations to create online experiences that are seamlessly accessible to all.

What We Do

A11y Web Design using the POOR Design Process

Our team specializes in A11y Web Design, leveraging the POOR Design process to develop websites that are not only visually appealing but also inherently compliant with accessibility standards. The POOR Design process prioritizes creating digital spaces that are Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, and Robust, ensuring inclusivity from the ground up.

A11y Audits by Certified DHS Trusted Testers

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey conducts comprehensive Accessibility Audits by a team of Certified DHS Trusted Testers. This certification ensures that our experts possess the necessary skills and knowledge to perform audits that meet the highest accessibility standards. We provide actionable insights to enhance accessibility and compliance with industry standards.

Proof of Compliance Certificates (POC)

Proof of Compliance Certificates from Kaffeinated Kodemonkey demonstrate our commitment to accessible design. Display our certification proudly and let your audience know that you prioritize inclusivity.

Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT)

We offer Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) services, ensuring that your digital products and services align with accessibility standards. Enhance your market presence by showcasing your commitment to accessibility.

A11y Monitoring with PopeTech

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey utilizes the PopeTech online A11y Monitoring Platform for in-house use by our clients. This platform provides real-time assessments and ongoing evaluations of your digital platforms, ensuring that your website maintains accessibility standards and remains user-friendly for everyone.
