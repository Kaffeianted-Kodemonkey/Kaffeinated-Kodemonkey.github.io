Kaffeinated Kodemonkey – Your Web Accessibility Expert

Founded by Heather to champion digital inclusivity, Kaffeinated Kodemonkey transforms compliance into opportunity. With expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, CMS platforms, and SSG, we craft accessible websites that meet WCAG 2.X standards, enhance search rankings, and attract diverse audiences. Our mission is to empower organizations and deliver user-focused solutions for clients and employers seeking top-tier accessibility expertise.

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey, led by Heather, a DHS Certified Trusted Tester and front-end developer with over a decade of experience, delivers cutting-edge web accessibility solutions. Specializing in Proof of Compliance Certificates (POCC), A11y Monitoring, and A11y-First Web Design, we ensure ADA, WCAG 2.X, and Section 508 compliance for businesses, government, and education. Whether you’re seeking contract expertise or accessibility services, our solutions reduce legal risks, boost SEO, and drive client growth through inclusive digital experiences.

Our Commitment

  • A11y-First Web Design: Creates custom, POUR-compliant websites optimized for SEO, accessibility, and user experience, driving client acquisition.

  • Certified Expertise: DHS Trusted Tester and IAAP credentials ensure rigorous, compliant deliverables for contract work or client projects.
  • Technical Proficiency: Over 10 years of front-end development experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and CMS platforms, delivering robust, accessible solutions.
  • SEO & Accessibility Integration: Combines compliance with SEO strategies to maximize visibility and user reach for clients or employers.
